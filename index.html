ABOUT OUR SHOP

Welcome to Muarya Cricket Chap — the heartbeat of cricket in Mahadewa, Siwan. Since our founding, we've been the go-to destination for cricketers of all levels, from gully warriors to serious club players across Bihar.

We stock a wide range of premium cricket equipment — bats, leather balls, tennis balls, batting gloves, pads, shoes, and complete jersey kits — all at prices that respect your budget without compromising on quality.

Every product we sell is hand-selected by cricket enthusiasts who understand the game. Come visit us at Mahadewa, Siwan, and experience the difference of a shop that lives and breathes cricket.
